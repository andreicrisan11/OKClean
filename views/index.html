What we do

OkClean is an UK based company offering the best professional cleaning products on the market. There is no type of material our chemicals can’t deal with due to a specific formula of targeting individual types of stains. Every product was especially conceived for a certain type of dirt covering the entire range of surfaces: metal, wood, glass or rock. OkClean is also the best alternative for home cleaning where common products are not effective and make you work twice as hard. Our products are being used for over 10 years in all types of business such as hotels, restaurants, offices, schools or hospitals delivering satisfaction and confidence.
